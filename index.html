10th Bramhapuri Online Blitz Chess Tournament

22 October 2021, Friday @ 8:30 PM IST
(For Indian Players Only)

Bramhapuri Taluka Chess Association (BTCA) has been organizing an online blitz chess event every month in the year 2021. We are back again with yet another online event in October.


The event boasts a prize fund of 21,000 INR and, Free Entry is, of course, a specialty of our online events.


Only Indian Players will be eligible to play and win the prizes of the tournament.
