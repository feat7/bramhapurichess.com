3rd Bramhapuri Online Blitz Chess Tournament

21 March 2021, Sunday @ 8:45 PM IST
(For Indian Players Only)

Note - The tournament timings are adjusted from 8:30 PM to 8:45 PM in the wake of requests from many players as there is another tournament that ends by 8:45 PM

Bramhapuri Taluka Chess Association (BTCA) has been organizing an online blitz chess event every month in the year 2021. We are back again with yet another online event in March.


The event boasts a prize fund of 27,000 INR and, Free Entry is, of course, a specialty of our online events.


There is a slight change in the 3rd event. We are experimenting that only Indian Players will be eligible to play and win the prizes of the tournament.
