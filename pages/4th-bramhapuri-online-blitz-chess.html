4th Bramhapuri Online Blitz Chess Tournament

(For Indian Players Only)

Bramhapuri Taluka Chess Association (BTCA) has been organizing an online blitz chess event every month in the year 2021. We are back again with yet another online event in April.


The event boasts a prize fund of 23,000 INR and, Free Entry is, of course, a specialty of our online events.


Only Indian Players will be eligible to play and win the prizes of the tournament.
