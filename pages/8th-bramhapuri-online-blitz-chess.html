8th Bramhapuri Online Blitz Chess Tournament

19 August 2021, Thursday @ 8:30 PM IST
(For Indian Players Only)


Final Ranking List Prize List

Bramhapuri Taluka Chess Association (BTCA) has been organizing an online blitz chess event every month in the year 2021. We are back again with yet another online event in August.


The event boasts a prize fund of 21,000 INR and, Free Entry is, of course, a specialty of our online events.


Only Indian Players will be eligible to play and win the prizes of the tournament.
